Deforestation & Zoonotic Spillover

The Rapid Destruction of Pristine Forests is a Direct Threat to Global Human Health.

The Buffer is Gone

As human activities push deeper into untouched ecosystems, the buffer zone between wildlife and human populations vanishes. Viruses that circulate harmlessly in reservoir species now find literal clear paths to livestock, and inevitably, to humans.

Adopt the "One Health" Approach

The "One Health" concept recognizes that human health is tightly linked to the health of animals and our shared environment. We cannot have a healthy human society on a dying planet.

  • Ecosystem Health: Intact forests safely contain diverse wildlife and pathogens.
  • Animal Health: Minimal stress and natural habitats prevent uncontrolled disease replication.
  • Human Health: Sustainable land management is the ultimate vaccine against future pandemics.
