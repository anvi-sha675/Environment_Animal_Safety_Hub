Reduction Tips

Reduce Your Food Waste

Practical tips to minimize food waste and maximize your resources.

Plan Your Meals

Create a weekly meal plan and shopping list dedicated to what you'll actually eat.

Smart Shopping

Buy only what you need. Check expiry dates and use FIFO (First In, First Out) method.

Proper Storage

Store food correctly at the right temperature to extend shelf life.

Composting

Start composting organic waste. It's great for your garden and the environment!

Share Surplus

Share excess food with neighbors, friends, or donate to local food banks.

Use Leftovers

Get creative with leftovers. Transform them into new, delicious meals.
